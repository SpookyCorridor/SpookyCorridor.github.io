Crumblr.

Complete a natural-sounding sentence to find something cool.

Process

Crumblr is a simple way to find wallpapers and videos from Reddit. It focuses on a rich user experience with the idea of natural language to utilize Reddit's advanced search criteria. I created Crumblr because I wanted a quick way to find new wallpapers or inspiration.

Crumblr works with two media formats. It searches through a query for imgur images and youtube videos depending on what the user wants. The challenge with Crumblr was figuring out a way to reasonably mull through posts in certain subreddits and find media which meets the user's needs.

Overview

  1. Choose a keyword to search for
  2. Choose a subreddit to search in
  3. Decide how popular your result should be
  4. Decide how far back you'd like to look
  5. (Wallpaper only) Set a minimum image size in pixels

Wallpapers

Find a new wallpaper based on dimensions, subreddit, and date.

Videos

Discover the next viral video before your friends.
